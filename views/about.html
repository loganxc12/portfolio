Hey, I’m Logan (the founder of Dawnland Creative). I started this small
company with the goal of helping fellow Mainers succeed with modern
websites. As someone who’s worked online for many years, I’m acutely
aware of the ever-increasing importance of digital strategy, and the
massive impact a professional website can make on business.

Based near the ocean in Rockport, it is my belief that effective design is
rooted in simplicity and clarity of message (not flashy animations and
complex interfaces), and I work with clients to distill their brand’s "voice"
so they connect with visitors and stand out online. While I specialize in
web design, I’m also experienced in copywriting and video production
and love the creative process of bringing a company’s vision to life.

The process of working with us is simple. We’ll start by gaining clarity on
your company’s message and goals, digging below the surface to uncover
what specific language and media will “hook” your target audience. From
that foundation of clarity and purpose, we’ll dive into the design process,
activating maximum creativity(!) to develop a site you adore. And finally,
once you’re 100% happy with the finished product, we’ll launch your
new website to the world. Have a project in mind? Browse through
our work and, if you like what you see, reach out!
